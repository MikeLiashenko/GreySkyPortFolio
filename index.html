Privacy Policy & Terms

Last Updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data across our ecosystem, including the StormBook mobile application and the GreySky portfolio website.


1. Data Collection & Firebase

Our applications use Firebase Realtime Database to store user-generated text data, storm logs, diary entries, radio messages, and standard text metadata. We do not collect or share personal identifiable information.


2. User Media & Metadata

When you upload photo proofs for severe storms, the application processes local image files. Any image data uploaded to the server is used solely for community leaderboards and verification logs.


3. Account Deletion

Users maintain full rights over their data. Both the website dashboard and mobile clients provide an irreversible "Delete Account" mechanism that instantly removes your associated records from our Firebase servers.


4. Third-Party Services

We utilize open-source and trusted third-party APIs (such as Jitsi Meet API for active encrypted voice/video communications). These platforms handle traffic independently.


If you have questions regarding this policy, contact the team via our official distribution nodes on GitHub or community channels.
