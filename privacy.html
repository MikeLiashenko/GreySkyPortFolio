Privacy Policy

StormBook · Weather Gang

🗓️ Last updated: July 2026

StormBook (“Weather Gang”) is part of the GreySky ecosystem. This document explains what data the app processes and how you can control it. By using StormBook, you agree to the terms below.

1 What data we collect

We use Firebase Authentication for sign-up and login and store your email address. Your callsign is the public name of your account. Firebase Realtime Database stores the content you create and your game state:

  • diary entries and storm logs (location, type, year, timestamps);
  • messages in the radio, private chats and rooms;
  • images: avatar, storm photo proofs, marketplace listings;
  • profile: avatar, description, equipped titles and frames;
  • in-app currency balance (⚡), purchases, listings, leagues and events;
  • moderation data: reports you send and users you have blocked.

The in-app currency ⚡ has no monetary value and cannot be exchanged for real money.

2 Public data

Your callsign, avatar, stats, diary entries and marketplace listings may be visible to other users — in the leaderboard, leagues, market and profile. The privacy setting affects how your storm entries are shown, but content sent to shared chats and rooms or listed on the market is visible to other participants.

3 Images and metadata

When you upload a photo, the app processes the file locally and reads EXIF metadata (e.g. camera model) for authenticity checks. Uploaded images are stored on the server and may contain the original file's metadata. Do not upload photos whose metadata (including geolocation) you do not wish to disclose. Images appear in the diary, profile, leaderboard and market.

4 Account deletion

Settings include a “Delete Account” function. It irreversibly deletes your profile, diary and Firebase Authentication account.

Note: messages previously sent to the radio, private chats and rooms, active marketplace listings and submitted reports may remain as part of shared history. Contact the team to have them removed.

5 Third-party services

The app uses Google Firebase (Realtime Database and Authentication) for storage and authentication, and the exif-js library (via the jsDelivr CDN) to read image metadata. Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS and processed under Google's terms. We do not use third-party advertising or analytics and we do not sell your data.

StormBook is part of the GreySky ecosystem — the developer's portfolio website. Links to current resources are published in the official distribution channels.

6 Children

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under this age.

7 Contact

For questions about this Policy, contact the developer at vitaliy.lyashenko@gmail.com.

🗑️ How to delete your StormBook account and data

In the app. Open StormBook, go to Settings → Delete Account, and confirm. This is the fastest route and needs no request.

By request. If you can no longer sign in, email vitaliy.lyashenko@gmail.com from the address linked to your account, with your callsign in the message. Requests are handled within 30 days.

What is deleted: your profile, avatar, diary entries and storm logs, in-app currency balance, inventory and marketplace listings, and your Firebase Authentication account (including your email address).

What may remain: messages you sent to the radio, private chats and rooms, and moderation records (reports, bans), because they are part of other users' shared history. These are retained indefinitely unless you ask for them to be removed, in which case they are deleted within 30 days of the request.
