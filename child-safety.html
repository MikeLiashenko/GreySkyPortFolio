Child Safety Standards

StormBook · GreySky

🗓️ Last updated: July 2026

Our commitment

StormBook is a community app for storm chasers. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and for any child sexual abuse material (CSAM). This page describes the standards we follow to keep minors safe, in line with Google Play's Child Safety Standards policy and applicable child-safety laws.

What is prohibited

The following are strictly forbidden on StormBook and result in immediate removal of content and permanent banning of the account:

  • any child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or content that sexualises minors;
  • grooming, solicitation, or sexual communication directed at minors;
  • sharing personal contact details to move a minor off-platform for abuse;
  • any other content or behaviour that endangers children.

How to report

In the app. Every user profile has a Report button (🚩). Use it to flag any user or content that violates these standards. Reports are reviewed by our moderators, who can remove content and ban accounts.

By email. You can also report child-safety concerns directly to vitaliy.lyashenko@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 24 hours and act on confirmed CSAE reports immediately.

How we respond

When a report is confirmed, we remove the content, permanently ban the responsible account, and preserve the relevant information. Apparent CSAM is reported to the appropriate authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) where applicable. Users can block other users at any time from a profile.

Compliance contact

Child-safety point of contact: vitaliy.lyashenko@gmail.com.

We comply with Google Play's Child Safety Standards policy and with applicable laws on preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation.
